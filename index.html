Story

Through this project, the Atypical are finally being considered as gifted and not ill or infirm. These characters, phenotypes and atypies should, on the contrary, be seen as rare identities and unique qualities.

Therefore, our duty became to preserve and perpetuate these singularities. Faced daily with the difficulties of life, the participation of the Atypical in the Metaverse must be strong and omnipresent in order to have a positive retrograde impact on everyday life.

We will build on this NFT Project to create a responsible, strong and inclusive community that will fight against all forms of segregation and body shaming.

We will seek to find appropriate solutions to the problems that atypical people may encounter in their daily life. Our Art collection will also not be limited to this collection since the atypies themselves know no limits. Our project will be rich and will evolve on two levels: an artistic digital plan and a real social plan.
